(Barista, server, librarian,lifeguard, and currently aveterinary nurse. There is one common thread stiching my life quilt together: people! I have learned to engage with a myriad of people & systems within the service industry, educational institutions, and medical establishments. Drawing from past and current experiences, I consider myself a technological medium. I believe the charm of UX design can be found in the close embrace of ethnographic studies and visual communication.
