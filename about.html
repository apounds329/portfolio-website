My Story

Originally from Fort Lauderdale, I'm currently a product designer based in Orlando, Florida.


I'm skilled in the end-to-end design process from researching to problem solving to prototyping, iterating, and testing. My goal is to bridge the gap between companies, users, and developers, allowing for seamless design and user expereince. Since studying film and psychology at the University of Central Florida, I have always been interested in the visual storytelling and the cognitive aspects of the human condition.


Coming from a background in the service industry and veterinary nursing, I understand the value of dedicating myself to understanding and serving others (both human and non-human). The same principles of advocacy and emptahy apply to UX design: the user is always front and center. Within each of my past roles, I designed and developed innovative protocols that were integrated into company function, increasing efficiency, safety, and comfort. I had no idea what I was doing at the time was actually a thing: user centered design.


When I'm not working, I enjoy traveling, flim, practicing various styles of hot yoga, eating delicious vegetarian foods, and hanging out at home with my wife, 3 dogs and 4 guinea pigs.
